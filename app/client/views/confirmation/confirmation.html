Travel

Our reimbursement policy is as follows:

  • Bus: We will be offering bus routes starting at Purdue, University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign, Georgia Institute of Technology and University of Florida. If you can find your own way to one of those stops, you can board the bus. We will require reservation of bus seats ahead of time (this will be released soon).
  • Car: We will generally reimburse all gas costs for cars containing 3 or more hackers. We may offer partial reimbursement to cars containing fewer hackers.
  • Flight: We will generally not be reimbursing flights. However, we may consider on a case by case basis small reimbursements (~$50) similar to the reimbursement hackers that drive would receive. Contact us for more information.

If you have another way to get to VandyHacks (or just have questions), email us at travel@vandyhacks.org.
